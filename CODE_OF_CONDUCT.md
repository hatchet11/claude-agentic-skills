# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This is a practical open-source project. Keep it useful, direct, and professional.

We welcome contributors from law enforcement, intelligence, development, content creation, emergency management, and adjacent fields. Differences in background and expertise make the skills here better.

Expected behavior:
- Be direct and constructive in reviews and discussions.
- Respect that contributors work in high-stakes fields — be precise about bugs and design concerns.
- Credit prior work and upstream sources.
- Keep discussions on topic: the skills, the tools, the craft.

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or personal attacks in any form.
- Sharing others' private information without consent.
- Submitting skills designed to cause harm, violate privacy, or circumvent legal constraints.
- Misrepresenting the origin or purpose of a contribution.

## Scope

This applies to all spaces associated with this repository: issues, pull requests, discussions, and any community spaces that reference this project.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to **hatchet412@proton.me**. Reports are handled confidentially. Maintainers will review and respond as appropriate — up to and including permanent removal from the project.
